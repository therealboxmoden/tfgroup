Trans-Fem*

We are a support, social, and resource group for transwomen, intersex, non-binary & questioning people who are looking to cultivate a more femme gender expression. We meet for fun, discussion & support around what it means to be on the trans-feminine spectrum. There are no requirements for transition status or presentation to attend this group.

Trans-Fem* is currently holding virtual and in-person support group meetings. For more information contact Danielle at dani@transfem.group
