Trans-Fem*

We are a support, social, and resource group for transwomen, intersex, non-binary & questioning people who were assigned male at birth. We meet for fun, discussion & support around what it means to be on the trans-feminine spectrum. There are no requirements for transition status or presentation to attend this group.

Trans-Fem* is having virtual meetings via zoom every Wednesday. For more information contact Danielle at dani@transfem.group
